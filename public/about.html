About

This website allows users to read up on all things cryptocurrency. Whether that means scanning articles about the latest coin openings from Business Insider or simply settling down with a long-form piece from CoinDesk, Crypto-Net News is your go-to article aggregator. Users can populate articles from any newspaper or magazine of their choice as well as study these dynamic markets using the ticker widget.


This website was built over the course of roughly two weeks by students at the Northwestern Coding Boot Camp. They employed a variety of technologies including MongoDB, Express, HTML5 and CSS3.
